The Festival of Colors: Holi 2025

Holi, the festival of colors, is celebrated on the full moon day in the month of Phalgun which usually falls in March. In 2025, Holi will be celebrated on [specific date]. This ancient Hindu festival marks the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil.

The festival begins with Holika Dahan, where people gather around a bonfire to celebrate the victory of good over evil. The next day is marked by playing with colors, water guns, and sharing sweets with friends and family.

Across different regions of India, Holi is celebrated with various customs and traditions, but the spirit of joy and togetherness remains the same everywhere.
