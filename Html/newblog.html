Blogging Se Paise Kamane Ke Tarike

1 Google AdSense

Blogger Google ka platform hai, toh aap Google AdSense ke liye apply kar sakte hain. Jab aapke blog par achha traffic aayega, toh aap AdSense se ads laga sakte hain aur har click par paise kama sakte hain.

2 Affiliate Marketing

Aap Amazon, Flipkart, Hostinger, Bluehost jaise affiliate programs join kar sakte hain. Apne blog par unke products ka review likh kar ya unke links share karke commission kama sakte hain.

3 Sponsored Posts

Jab aapka blog popular ho jaye, toh companies aapko paise dekar apne products ka review ya promotion likhva sakti hain.

4 Digital Products Bechna

Aap E-books, Courses, Templates, PDF Guides bech sakte hain aur apne blog se revenue generate kar sakte hain.

5 Freelancing ya Services

Agar aap kisi skill me expert hain (jaise content writing, web designing, SEO), toh aap apne blog ke through clients attract kar sakte hain.
